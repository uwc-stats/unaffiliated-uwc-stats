    • What exactly does the algorithm do?

    The algorithm cleans up the raw misspelled or variant words to a known keyword.

    Raw Keyword
    Lewis & Clark College -> Lewis and Clark College
    Vietnam -> Viet Nam
    UWC South East Asia -> South East Asia
    Massachusetts Institute of Technology -> MIT

    How do we choose the keyword?

  • Country keywords are taken from ISO 3166 through the python library pycountry 22.3.5

    • Minor manual correction are needed for data with previous country name such as "Swaziland" to "Eswatini".

    To accurately reflect the raw data, we have to manually add in additional keywords to the accomand for some geographical region(s).

    We do not intend to be offend, deceive or misrepresent any party. Please contact us for constructive feedback regarding appropriate update for these keywords.
